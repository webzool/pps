Overview

Gusto /ˈɡəstō/ noun: meaning eagerness, enjoyment, fervor, — and now — payroll.

Launched in 2012 as ZenPayroll, Gusto is an easy to use payroll solution designed to simplify complicated business tasks and let owners get back to doing what they love. The software serves over 60,000 businesses across the US and has gained much acclaim, including a spot in Forbes Fintech 50 for 2018.

Gusto is a cloud-based payroll solution that seamlessly combines benefits administration, 401(k)s, HR, and onboarding. The company takes care of payroll calculations and tax filing for you and even has an automatic payroll service called Gusto AutoPilot, saving many business owners time and stress. Gusto is valid in all 50 states and works for companies with employees in multiple states. Add great customer support and up-front, affordable pricing to all of this and its easy to see why Gusto receives so many positive reviews form its customers.

Like any software, Gusto isn’t without faults. There is limited reporting and limited time tracking and health benefits are not available in all 50 states yet. However, this payroll solution still has a ton to offer and is more than worth looking into.
